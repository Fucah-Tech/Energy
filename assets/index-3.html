Ashfar Power SARL

Ashfar Power SARL is part of the ASHFAR Group which is committed to consistently providing reliable energy solutions that meet and/or exceed our customer expectations in rural and peri urban communities.

Across the African continent, current power supply is inadequate. According to the International Energy Association, more than 590 million people are without access to electricity.
